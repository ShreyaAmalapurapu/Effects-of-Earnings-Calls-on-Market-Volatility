Feature Justifications
============================================================

MARKET FEATURES:
  CR_0_5/30/120: Cumulative log returns over post-call windows — captures directional price impact of earnings.
  RV_pre/0_30/0_120: Realized volatility (sqrt sum r²) — measures uncertainty resolution around the call.
  VOL_ratio: Post/pre volume ratio — captures change in trading activity triggered by the call.
  max_runup/drawdown: Extreme cumulative moves — captures asymmetry in price reaction.
  time_to_max_abs_move: Minutes until peak absolute move — captures how quickly information is priced in.

TRANSCRIPT FEATURES:
  word_count_total: Total transcript length — proxy for information density and call complexity.
  qa_ratio: Q&A share of total — higher ratio → more analyst engagement.
  num_questions: Count of questions — direct measure of analyst scrutiny.
  tone (LM dictionary): (pos - neg) / total — net sentiment using Loughran-McDonald financial lexicon.
  uncertainty_rate: uncertainty words / total — captures forward-looking hedging language.
  topic_1..20: TF-IDF + SVD latent topics — captures thematic content without manual labeling.

NORMALIZATION:
  All numeric features z-scored: z = (x - mean) / std.
  Ticker one-hot encoded for categorical representation.
